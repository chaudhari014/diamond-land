Online Jewellery Store


DiamondLand.com began in 2008 with the simple motto of democratising jewellery. And today. after 13 glorious years, it has come to be recognised as one of the top 20 e-commerce portals in India. Steadily growing from strength to strength since inception. DiamondLand has also established 165 stores across the length and breadth of India.


Since July 2016, we joined force with India's most desired and largest jewellery brand. Tanishq, through a strategic investment made by Ttan Company in DiamondLand. This takes us another step closer to achieving our shared missing - to make beautiful jewellery accessible to everyone.
